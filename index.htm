Our Story

It all started the best way possible — at the dog park. Matt spotted Chelsea sitting on a bench with her dog, Ryder, and struck up a conversation. Before long, he invited her out with friends that same night. As the night went on, Matt's dog, Titan, made himself right at home by Chelsea's side — and that's when Matt knew she was special. What began as a simple invitation quickly turned into something neither of them expected. From wagging tails to a forever kind of love, their story is one they'll always be grateful for.

❧

After purchasing their brand-new RV, Chelsea took a leap of faith and quit her job while Matt continued working remotely. Together, they set off to travel across the country and began a new chapter on the road. From glowing desert sunsets to peaceful mountain views, they discovered new dog parks, created lasting memories and grew even closer along the way. Life on the road brought them a deep sense of freedom, adventure and connection that they will always carry with them.

❧

Today, Chelsea and Matt stand hand in hand, ready to begin their forever. What started as a simple moment — thanks to two dogs who knew they belonged together long before they did — has grown into a love filled with laughter, adventure and an unbreakable bond. Through every mile traveled and every memory made, they have found home in one another. Now, surrounded by those they love most, they step into this next chapter — heart full, side by side and forever intertwined.

With love, Chelsea & Matt
